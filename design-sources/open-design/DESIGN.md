# Axis
> Category: Product
> Workflow-driven business application platform for data models, workflows, forms, and pages.

## Direction

Calm, precise, operational.

## Screen Rules

- Prioritize scanning, comparison, editing, validation, and recovery.
- Use quiet surfaces, crisp borders, readable density, and restrained elevation.
- Use neutral work areas with teal primary actions and amber accents.
- Use color only for state, priority, or action.
- Keep corners modest; panel radius max 8px.
- Design mobile first; account and navigation actions stay reachable.
- Avoid decorative gradients, hero cards, floating ornaments, fake metrics, and one-note palettes.

## Type And Copy

- Use compact labels, clear headings, and short helper copy.
- Use restrained heading sizes inside work surfaces.
- Do not scale type with viewport width; keep letter spacing normal.
- Long English and Vietnamese labels wrap or truncate intentionally.
- Keep product copy short, specific, and action-oriented.
- Prefer product words: "data model", "workflow", "execution", "form", "page", "workspace".

## Handoff

Design artifacts are upstream evidence. They do not create product behavior, acceptance criteria, roles, endpoints, data contracts, or runtime APIs.

Translate approved visual decisions through the owning use case and design-system workflow. Do not paste generated HTML/CSS into the SPA or replace editable design sources with export-only assets.
